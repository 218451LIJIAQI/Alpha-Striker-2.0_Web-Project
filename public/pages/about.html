Our Story

From Vision to Reality

Alpha Striker 2.0 began with a simple idea: to create a seamless bridge between moviegoers and theaters. Founded in 2015 by a group of film enthusiasts and tech innovators, we set out to transform the way people experience cinema.

What started as a small startup with a handful of partner theaters has now grown into the nation's leading movie ticketing platform, serving millions of movie lovers across 500+ cities.

Our journey has been driven by a passion for innovation and a deep love for the cinematic experience. We've continuously evolved our platform to make movie discovery and ticket booking as simple and enjoyable as watching the movie itself.
