Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How do I register a new account?
- Click Sign Up in the top-right corner of the homepage.
- Fill in your Full Name, Email, and Password, then agree to the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.
- Click Create Account. You’ll be automatically logged in and redirected to the Movies page.
2. What if I forget my password?
- On the Sign In page, click Forgot Password?
- Enter the email address you used to register.
- Click Reset Password and check your inbox for a reset link. Follow the link to choose a new password.
3. How do I search for and book a movie?
- After signing in, click Movies in the top navigation.
- Use the search bar to enter a movie title, theater name, or keyword, then click Search.
- Find your desired film in the results and click Book Now.
- Select your Date, Show Time, and Seats, then click Proceed to Payment to complete your booking.
4. How do I find theaters near me?
- Click Theaters in the top menu.
- Enter your city or ZIP code and click Search.
- Filter results by format (e.g. IMAX, 4DX, VIP) if you wish.
- Click View Theater Details to see the address, amenities, and available showtimes.
5. How can I use a promo code or coupon?
- Go to the Offers page to view all active promotions.
- Click Copy on the offer card to copy its code.
- On the payment page, paste the code into the Promo Code field and click Apply to redeem your discount.
6. What is the membership program and how does it work?
Earning Points: You earn points on every ticket purchase.
Tier Levels: Points accumulate to upgrade you through Silver, Gold, and Platinum tiers.
Member Benefits: Each tier unlocks perks such as extra discounts, priority seating, and free concessions.
Check your current status under My Profile → Membership.
7. Which payment methods are accepted?
- Major credit/debit cards
- PayPal
- Apple Pay
- Grab Pay
Payment is processed instantly; your e-ticket will be available as soon as the transaction succeeds.
8. How do I use my e-ticket?
- After payment, go to Shopping cart icon in the upper right corner.
- Open the relevant order and display the barcode or QR code on your device.
- Present it at the theater entrance for scanning - no printing required.
9. Can I cancel or change my booking?
Cancellations: Once a ticket is purchased, cancellations are supported through the E-Ticket UI.
Rescheduling: To change your showtime, please contact support at least 2 hours before the original showtime.
